# Privacy Policy for Sky Plane Dash

Sky Plane Dash is a simple arcade game created for entertainment purposes.  
The game does NOT collect or store any personal user data.

## Information Collection
We do not collect:
- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Location
- Device ID
- Any personal information

## Advertising
The game uses third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob).  
These services may collect anonymous data for ad personalization or analytics.  

Please check Google’s privacy policy for details:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children’s Privacy
Sky Plane Dash does not target children specifically and does not collect any personal information from minors.
